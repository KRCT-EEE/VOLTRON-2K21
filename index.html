VOLTRON'21

Beyond the barriers

With the objective to provide a limelight platform for student community to expose their Technical and Non-technical skills, VOLTRON'21 is aforethought. VOLTRON'21 is an national level technical symposium which is a Virtual talent hunt for young superminds, conducted at the month of November by the ELEKTRA Association of Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, K.Ramakrishnan College Of Technology (Autonomous). VOLTRON'21 will be an virtual fete for all those Technical and non - technical quester. Yes! You are right. Fete filled with technical, non - technical events and what not. So all, clear your plans and get ready to enjoy and welcome VOLTRON'21!

General Rules

  • Every participant is expected to behave politely with organisers, volunteers and other participants.
  • Misconduct or potential malpractice in any form will be penalised and may include disqualification and/or ban.
  • Participants are advised to be present at the respective meeting platforms in advance to avoid last-minute hassles. Any request for a delay will not be entertained.
  • It is the participant's responsibility to have a stable internet connection. Dealing with any discontinuity in the event on the participant's side is not the organisers responsibility.
  • Participants must further abide by the specific rules of the individual competitions/events which are stated in the rule book

General Rules

  • The registration amount for participating in VOLTRON'21 events for Free.
  • All events will be conducted through Online mode
  • Prior Registration is mandatory for all events.
  • Individual Participant / Team should adhere to the rules and regulations of respective event.
  • Student should attend the events in their Institution prescribed dress code and produce ID card issued by the respective institute whenever requested..
  • Event Coordinator has all rights to disqualify any participant if not adhere with the general etiquettes of the contest..
  • Decisions of the Judges will be unbiased and considered as final.
  • E- Certificates will be provided to all participants

General Rules

  • Participants should send their works to voltron2k21@gmail.com
  • Name of the participant, event name, department, college name should be added in the mail
  • Participants are kindly requested to give your details with care to prompt delivery of Your certificates
  • The attachments are preferably to be as Images and Videos
  • The winners selection method will be mentioned in the event rules separately.
  • E-certificates will be provided to all the participants
  • Exclusive cash prizes for the winners
  • One can participate in many events
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited, if found then the entry will be disqualified and won’t receive any E-certificates

The SlideShow

EVENT RULES
  • Maximum number of participants in a team is 2.
  • Papers need to be sent in the specified & suggested areas only.
  • Please mail your abstract to the respective mail-id.
  • The mail id should contain the following:
    1. Title of the Paper
    2. Name of the Participants
  • Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should not exceed 10 pages.
  • The teams will get 10 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
  • There should be minimum of 10 slides and it should not exceed 20.
  • TOPICS:
    1. All engineering related topics will be accepted

Tech Thoughts

EVENT RULES
  • Your presentation should be 15 minutes long with time (5 minutes) for questions afterward.
  • Maximum number of participants is 2. Each member of your group (if your project is a group project) must play a significant role in the presentation.
  • Please mail your abstract along with objective of the project not exceeding 1 page to the respective mail-id.
  • The mail id should contain the following:
    1. Title of the Paper along with a 1 line objective.
    2. Name of the Participants
    3. Email Id & Mobile Number of the Authors
    4. Year of study, department and College of the Authors.
  • Start with an outline and end with a conclusion. At the beginning of your presentation, let your panel members know what to expect; at the end, concisely review what you’ve done
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

On the Mic

EVENT RULES
  • The participants shall register themselves through online.
  • Each participant will be given a time limit not exceeding 5 minutes to speak on the subject. A warning bell will be given after 4 minutes and a final bell after 5 minutes.
  • Participant has to speak only in English and the participants will be allowed to select their own topic but not about any individuals, religion, Politics etc.,.
  • A Copy of Content should be sent to the coordinator two days before the event for verification and acceptance.
  • TOPICS:
    1. Is covid-19 is Natural or Artificial Virus
    2. Is Privatization is Good for our country
    3. rich get richer and the poor get poorer

LensMan

EVENT RULES
  • PLAGIARISM is strictly prohibited. If found that entry will be disqualified.
  • Photo can be taken either in DSLR or Mobile phone.
  • Participants can choose only one topic among given topics.
  • Basic editing is only allowed.
  • Only one entry is allowed per participants.
  • Participants should submit two images (Straight out of camera image, edited image)
  • The image should convey a message or story.
  • A caption(not more than 20 words) is to be submitted along with image.
  • Photos submitted must be at least 640 pixels on the shorter side, and no more than 2000 pixels on the longer side. Images should be no larger than 2MB. Photos must be in JPEG format..
  • TOPICS:
    1. Monochromatic Photography.
    2. Urban and Rural life photography.
    3. Vintage Photography.
